One-sample t-test

\[t = \frac{\bar{Y}-\mu_{0}}{SE_{\bar{Y}}}\]

\(\mu_0\) = population mean (under \(H_0\))

\(\bar{Y}\) = sample mean

\(SE_{\bar{Y}}\) = sample SE

One-sample t-test

\(H_0\): \(\mu\) = 37

t.test(beaver1$temp, mu = 37)
